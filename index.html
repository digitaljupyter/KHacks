Peshka

Peshka is Russian for Pawn in Chess. A Pawn is the simplest unit in Chess therefore The name "Peshka" is used for the simple RAT program (might not be considered a RAT per-say.)

This program basically takes a screenshot of the victim's current screen when they run the program and sends it to a Discord WebHook, now along with the screenshot it also sends the victim's IP address, now however this is not original whatsoever. Therefore it is named after a Pawn due to the simple nature of the program. It is definitely The least dangerous but is mainly used in Ransomware attacks such as the series of Sextortion attacks.
